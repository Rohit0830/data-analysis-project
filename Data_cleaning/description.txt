Data cleaning (or data cleansing/scrubbing) is the crucial process of finding, fixing, or removing errors, inconsistencies, and inaccuracies in raw datasets to improve data quality, making it accurate, complete, and reliable for analysis and decision-making. It involves tasks like handling missing values, correcting typos, removing duplicates, standardizing formats, and filtering outliers, ensuring trustworthy insights rather than flawed outcomes from "dirty data"
